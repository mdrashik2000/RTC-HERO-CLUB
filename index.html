FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS.

Everything you need to know before joining the RTC community.

Can I trade any market with these indicators?
Absolutely. The RTC Indicator Suite is universally designed to work flawlessly across ALL markets and asset classes. Whether you trade global equities, forex pairs, crypto assets, or futures/indices, our algorithms adapt to any price action and time frame seamlessly.
What platform does the RTC Indicator Suite work on?
The RTC Indicator Suite is built for TradingView. You'll need a TradingView account (free or paid) to access and use the indicators. Once your membership is verified, you'll receive an invite link to add the tools directly to your charts.
How do I get access after payment?
After payment via Whop, access is granted automatically within minutes. For crypto or bank transfers, join our Discord and message Admin Rashik with your proof of payment. Access is typically verified within a few hours.
Is there a refund policy?
Due to the digital nature of the RTC Indicator Suite, all sales are final once access is granted. We cannot offer refunds, exchanges, or cancellations under any circumstances.
Do I need to know ICT concepts to use the suite?
Some familiarity with ICT concepts like FVGs, CISD, and order blocks will help. However, our 3-part masterclass series on YouTube covers everything you need to configure and use the indicators from scratch.
Is my payment information secure?
Absolutely. Whop payments are processed via Stripe and are fully encrypted. For crypto payments, we use Chainalysis hash filtering to screen all incoming transactions.
